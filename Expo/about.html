We connect people,
we create opportunities.

At Senya, we believe communication should be accessible to everyone. That's why we created an inclusive platform that connects users with interpreters in real time, removing barriers for people with hearing disabilities in a fast and secure way.

Communication is a right, not a privilege.

Our story

SENYA was created to make communication in banks, health centers, and essential services more accessible for everyone.

Our mission

Provide an accessible, fast, and reliable solution that allows people to communicate through interpreters and technological tools in real time.

Our vision

Build a future where communication has no barriers and where everyone can access services and opportunities under equal conditions.

  • Inclusion
  • Trust
